Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers with FAQs

  • 1. Where do you source your spices from?

    Our spices are sourced from trusted suppliers and farmers who follow sustainable and ethical farming practices, located in regions known for rich spice cultivation.

  • 2. How should I store my spices?

    Store spices in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight, in airtight containers to maintain freshness and potency.

  • 3. Are your spices gluten-free?

    Yes, our spices are naturally gluten-free and processed in facilities that handle only spices to avoid cross-contamination.

  • 4. Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping fees and delivery times vary by location, and customs regulations may affect delivery times.

  • 5. Can I buy your spices in bulk?

    Yes, we offer bulk purchasing options for businesses with special pricing. Contact us for details.

  • 6. Are your spices organic?

    Many of our spices are organic and meet strict certification standards. Look for the organic label on specific products.

  • 7. How can I contact customer service?

    Contact us via email and phone given in the website, or through our website contact form. We try to respond within a week.

  • 8. Are your spices free from artificial additives?

    Yes, our spices are 100% natural and free from artificial additives, preservatives, and colors.
